Species Detail

Red Mangrove

Rhizophora mangle

Saltwater Coastal Prop Roots

Scientific Description

The Red Mangrove is easily distinguished from other mangrove species by its specialized aerial prop roots which extend from the trunk and lower branches, providing stability in soft, anaerobic soils. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and leathery, with a dark green upper surface and a paler underside. It is highly adapted to saline environments, actively excluding salt at the root level.
